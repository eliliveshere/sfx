Our Story

About SFX LED

Who We Are

SFX LED builds professional wireless reactive lighting for DJs, touring artists, rental companies, and event production. We started with a simple idea: stage lighting should be as wireless as everything else.

No cable runs. No DMX headaches. No setup that takes longer than soundcheck. Just powerful light that moves with your music — from the first beat to the last.

What We Build

Our products are designed for people who work in live performance — people who need gear that sets up fast, runs all night, packs down in minutes, and doesn't fail mid-show.

  • Wireless RGBW tube kits with 500+ sound-reactive animations
  • WLED-flashed DMX tubes for advanced builders and DIY rigs
  • Hard carry cases engineered for touring and rental use
  • 2-year warranty on all SFX RGBW kits

Who Uses SFX LED

Our kits are used by touring DJs, festival stages, nightclub installers, and AV rental companies who need reliable, road-ready wireless lighting that earns its keep every show.

Whether you're lighting a 50-person private event or a 5,000-person festival stage, SFX LED scales with your rig.
